Cookies Policy

Last updated: 01 Nov 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device to make the site work, improve performance, or remember preferences. Similar technologies include localStorage and pixels.

2. How we use cookies

  • Strictly necessary: required for security and basic site features.
  • Performance/Analytics: measure visits and usage (only if you consent).
  • Embedded media: players such as SoundCloud may set their own cookies.

4. Third‑party cookies

When you load a SoundCloud embed or follow external links (e.g., Instagram), those services may place cookies. Refer to their policies.

5. Managing cookies

You can change your preferences at any time on the Cookies Settings page, or via your browser settings.
